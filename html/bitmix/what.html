What is Bitshuffle?

Bitshuffle offers a free, secure place to store your bitcoins. When you're ready to make a transaction choose a recipient and let us send your bitcoins confidentially.

When sending your bitcoins Bitshuffle uses a combination of timing, redirects and temporary addresses to keep your transaction anonymous.
